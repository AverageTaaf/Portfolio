About Me

I'm Taafeef, a 16-year-old developer and student from Bangladesh who loves creating digital experiences that look sleek and run smooth. My sweet spot is frontend & UI/UX—HTML, CSS, JavaScript, plus design tools like Figma, Photoshop, and Illustrator—but I'm also exploring backend and AI to round out my skills.

I've built a weather app, Pomodoro timer, multi-page responsive portfolio, and advanced Roblox Studio UIs like an Admin Panel and Difficulty Chart Obby. I design first in a dedicated sketchbook, then code with a focus on clean visuals and performance.

I spend 3–4+ focused hours a day learning and building, and I thrive on collaboration—whether it's with seasoned devs or beginners. My goal is to create tech that actually matters, not just chase big-name logos.

Outside code, I'm into Formula 1, cars, bikes, gym, and journaling—the same energy of speed and precision drives every project I take on.
